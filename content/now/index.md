## March 2026

Work is intense in a good way. We're in the middle of rethinking how the Glovo app handles navigation between sections — trying to make the experience feel more fluid without breaking the mental model customers already have. A lot of whiteboard sessions, a lot of Compose code.

Outside of work I've been thinking more seriously about writing. I want to publish more consistently — not just technical pieces but also things adjacent to software: teams, decision-making, the craft of building products. This website is part of that push.

I've also started running again after a winter hiatus. Short distances, no pressure. Barcelona makes it easy.

**Reading:** *The Pragmatic Programmer* (rereading it, finding new things), and a book on urban planning I picked up on a whim.

## September 2025

Just came back from a few weeks off — properly off, no laptop. Did some hiking in the Dolomites and remembered why I moved to Europe in the first place.

At work we'd just wrapped up the Backend Driven UI project and were starting to see it pay off. The first feature shipped without a mobile release. That felt good.

I was also exploring the GenAI space more seriously — experimenting with how language models could fit into developer tooling rather than just end-user products. Nothing production-ready, but a lot of interesting prototypes.

**Reading:** *Thinking in Systems* by Donella Meadows. One of those books that changes how you see everything.
