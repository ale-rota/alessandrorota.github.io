I'm a Software Engineer with a focus on Android and mobile architecture. I grew up in Italy and have been living in Barcelona for the past few years, drawn here by the engineering scene and the weather.

My path into software was driven by a genuine curiosity for logic and systems — the kind of curiosity that makes you want to understand not just *what* works, but *why* it works and how it could work better. That's still what gets me going.

## Glovo — Senior Android Engineer
*Barcelona · 2022 – present*

I joined Glovo as part of the mobile team working on the core shopping experience. Most of my time here has been spent on architecture and the kind of problems that sit at the intersection of product ambition and technical constraints.

One of the projects I'm most proud of is the work we did on **Backend Driven UI** — an architecture that lets the server define what the mobile client renders, without requiring a new app release. It gave product teams much faster iteration cycles and reduced the coordination overhead between mobile and backend. I wrote about the approach in an [article on the Glovo tech blog](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alessandro-rota-2b1257134_backend-driven-ui-architecture-at-glovo-activity-7413976793587761152-Xroc).

I also worked on the **New Store Wall** — the interface customers see when browsing a supermarket or store in the app — and led the animation work using Jetpack Compose to make it feel fast and polished.

Beyond features, I've been involved in hiring, mentoring, and the occasional GenAI hackathon.
