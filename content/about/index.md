## Work

### Senior Software Engineer at Delivery Hero
*Barcelona, Spain · Sep 2025 – present*

This isn't a real company change. I joined the Order Tracking team that reports directly to Delivery Hero, the holding company of Glovo.
We built from scratch a new order tracking experience in the form of an SDK that is being integrated in all the seven food delivery apps owned by DH.

### Senior Software Engineer at Glovo
*Barcelona, Spain · Jan 2022 – Aug 2025*

I joined Glovo as Software Engineer in the Shopping Experience team. I contributed to modernize the codebase, add mobile observability and implement new features.

Later I switched to Store Wall team. Here I led a project to completely rebuild the store feed screen in Jetpack Compose and using Backend Driven UI.
This is one of the projects I'm most proud of. I also wrote an [article on the Glovo tech blog](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alessandro-rota-2b1257134_backend-driven-ui-architecture-at-glovo-activity-7413976793587761152-Xroc) about it.

In 2024 I was promoted to Senior Software Engineer.

Beyond features, I've been involved in hiring, mentoring and hackathons.

### Software Engineer at Iriscube Reply
*Turin, Italy · May 2018 – Dec 2021*

I worked on the mobile app of Intesa Sanpaolo, the biggest bank in Italy.
